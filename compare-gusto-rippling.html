We tested both. Here is the short version: If you have under 50 employees and no IT department, choose Gusto. If you need to ship laptops to remote employees, choose Rippling.

Head-to-Head Comparison

Feature Gusto Rippling
Pricing Transparency Transparent ($40/mo base) Hidden (Quote based)
Device Management (MDM) No Yes (Excellent)
International Contractors Good Great (EOR Native)
Setup Speed Fast (DIY) Medium (Sales required)

Our Final Verdict

Gusto feels like a friendly assistant. It is cute, easy, and rarely breaks. Rippling feels like an operating system. It is powerful, complex, and connects everything in your company.
