Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

At Vett India, we take privacy seriously. We verify users to ensure trust, not to sell your personal data.

1. What We Collect

We collect your Work Email and LinkedIn Profile URL solely for the purpose of verifying that you are a real employee at an Indian company.

2. Data Usage

Your individual review data is anonymized before being published. We do not display your name or email publicly. We do not sell your contact information to software vendors without your explicit consent.

3. The Voucher

Your email is used to send the Amazon Voucher. Once the voucher is sent, you can request data deletion at any time.
